•ABOUT

Vivir Studios is a photography and cinematography studio in Iloilo City, where timeless elegance and innovation work at their best.


At Vivir Studios we don’t just capture images; we capture stories, emotions, and journeys. Whether you’re preparing for a pre-debut or pre-wedding shoot, or seeking a professional corporate approach, our unique blend of classic charm and contemporary style ensures every frame stands out.


With a cinematic touch, we bring your memories to life in a way that only Vivir Studios can.
